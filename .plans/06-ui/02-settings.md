# Settings

## Scope

Settings centralizes org configuration.

Tabs:

- Organization.
- Team.
- Categories.
- Branding.
- Payments.
- Webhooks.
- Email Templates.
- Plan Limits.

## Organization

- Name.
- Slug, read-only initially.
- Contact email.
- Public booking URL copy/open controls.

## Team

- Members list.
- Invite member.
- Change role.
- Remove member.

## Categories

- Add/remove categories.
- Configure colors/icons.
- Categories drive event filters and public page styling.

## Branding

- Logo upload or URL.
- Public page preview later.

## Payments

- Available providers.
- Connect/disconnect Stripe.
- Show connected status.
- Currency selection.

## Webhooks

- Webhook URL.
- Signing secret.
- Regenerate secret.
- Delivery logs.
- Retry failed deliveries.

## Email Templates

- Template editor.
- Preview.
- Reset to default.
